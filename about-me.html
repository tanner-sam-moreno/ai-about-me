About Me

Tanner Samuel Moreno

My name is Tanner Moreno and I am a wannabe Graphic Designer. I took an interest into Graphic Design growing up watching NASCAR as a kid, always being fascinated by the paint schemes and the cool graphic tees that went along with each drivers merchandise. As a teenager I started to learn more about what Graphic Design was, and took a liking to artwork from Sam Bass, Ryan Williams, and Lue Creative. I finally was able to get my hands on Adobe apps when I turned 18 and taught myself how to use Photoshop and Illustrator, learning how to do T-Shirt Illustration using Vectors, as well as Poster and Social Media Graphics in photoshop.
